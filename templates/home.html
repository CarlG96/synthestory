Welcome to SyntheStory!

Got Writer's Block?

SyntheStory can solve that for you! Here at SyntheStory you can signup and start generating ideas today!

How to Use SyntheStory:

  1. Start by signing up for an account here:
  2. After logging in, click on the Create Idea tab in the menu above.
  3. Select which genre you would like to have a story idea created in.
  4. Refresh by hitting the Generate Another until you like the story idea (you can also type in whatever you want up to a certain amount of characters). Then give your story idea a title and hit Save Idea
  5. You'll be redirected to the My Stories page, where you can see your story ideas you've saved.
  6. You can then edit or delete story ideas you want by clicking on the Edit Idea or Delete Idea buttons.
