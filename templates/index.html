Welcome to SyntheStory!

Got Writer's Block?

SyntheStory can solve that for you! Here at SyntheStory you can signup and start generating ideas today!

How to Use SyntheStory:

Step 1

{% if not user.is_authenticated %}

Start by signing up for an account here: Account Signup

{% else %}

You've already completed this step by logging in!

{% endif %}

Step 2

After logging in, click on the Create Idea tab in the menu above.

Step 3

Select which genre you would like to have a story idea created in.

Step 4

Refresh by hitting the Generate Another until you like the story idea (you can also type in whatever you want as long as it's short). Then give your story idea a title and hit Save Idea.

Step 5

You'll be redirected to the My Stories page, where you can see a list of story ideas you've saved.

Step 6

From here, click on the View link to see that specific story idea.

Step 7

You can then edit or delete story ideas you want by clicking on the Edit Story Idea or Delete Story Idea buttons.
